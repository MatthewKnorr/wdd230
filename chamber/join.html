Membership Benefits:

  • NP Membership is for non-profit organizations and is free.
  • Bronze Membership - Increased benefits.
  • Silver Membership - More benefits.
  • Gold Membership - Highest level with the most benefits.

🌿 NP Membership - No Fee 🌿

💰 Cost: Free

Tailored for non-profit organizations, the NP Membership is a cost-free option that opens doors to a supportive community.

🎁 Membership Rewards:

  • Access to a network of like-minded non-profit entities.
  • Exclusive resources for organizational development.
  • Participation in collaborative initiatives.

🔶 Bronze Membership - $25 🔶

Elevate your experience with the Bronze Membership, offering enhanced benefits for those seeking a more robust engagement.

🎁 Membership Rewards:

  • Priority access to educational workshops and events.
  • Increased visibility within the community.
  • Exclusive content and early access to new features.
  • Everything from the NP Membership.

⚪ Silver Membership - $45 ⚪

Take your involvement to the next level with the Silver Membership, designed for individuals or entities desiring a comprehensive suite of benefits.

🎁 Membership Rewards:

  • Advanced networking opportunities with industry professionals.
  • Premium resources for skill development.
  • Recognition through featured spotlights.
  • Everything from the Bronze Membership.

🌟 Gold Membership - $55 🌟

The Gold Membership represents the pinnacle of engagement, offering unparalleled benefits for those seeking the highest level of support and recognition.

🎁 Membership Rewards:

  • VIP access to premium events and conferences.
  • Personalized consultations with industry experts.
  • Prominent visibility as a leader in the community.
  • Everything from the Silver Membership.
