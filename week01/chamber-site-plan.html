Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Purpose

The Orem Chamber of Commerce's website serves several key purposes:

  1. Support local economic growth and development.
  2. Facilitate leadership and community engagement.
  3. Promote and advocate for the interests of businesses and the community.
  4. Organize networking and educational events for members.
  5. Provide valuable resources and benefits to chamber members.

Scenarios

Scenarios represent questions from site visitors

  1. What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  2. How can I join the chamber and become a member?
  3. What are the dates and details of upcoming community events?
  4. Are there opportunities for advertising and promoting my business through the chamber?
  5. Where can I find the chamber's contact information, including phone numbers and email addresses?
